About Us

Pasculli Enterprises Pty Ltd is owned and operated by John Pasculli and his team, who offer top quality, cost effective asphalt work.

The company has been operating since 1984 and has completed many works for well known organisations within South Australia. Pasculli Enterprises is a member of the Civil Contractors Federation. The company works under the CCF Integrated Management System and holds the DPTI prequalification to perform A1 asphalt works. John Pasculli prides himself on honest, professional and quality customer service.
